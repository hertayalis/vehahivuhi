Saweetie’s Career Highlights:

  • Saweetie started her career uploading short raps on her Instagram account: @saweetie in 2016.
  • One of her videos showing her rapping on the beats of Khia’s classic, “My Neck, My Back(Lick it)” went viral after which she rose to popularity. The same song later got released as”Icy Grl” on her SoundCloud in the summer of 2017.
  • Soon after the release, a well-known producer and A&R executive, Max Gousse became her manager.
  • In October 2017, she released a video for her song, “Focus”.
  • In January 2018, she was named Tidal’s Artist of the Week and one of Pigeons & Planes’ Best New Artists of the Month.
  • She was also featured in an ad for Rihanna’s cosmetic company, “Fenty Beauty”.
  • In February 2018, she was signed to Warner Records and her manager Max Gousse’s record label Artistry Worldwide.
  • On March 16, 2018, Saweetie released her debut extended play, “High Maintenance” including 9 tracks such as “Intro, “B.A.N”, “Good Good”, “Icy Grl”, “High Maintainance”, “23”, “Respect”, and “Too Many”. The album was later certified platinum in September 2019.
  • On March 29, 2019, she released her second major-label EP, “Icy”. Its first single, “My Type”, was written by Saweetie that also debuted on Billboard Hot 100.
  • Saweetie later collaborated with “PrettyLittleThing” to launch a 59-piece clothing collection, “PrettyLittleThing x Saweetie”, in September 2019.
  • On January 28, 2020, she appeared as a guest performer on the show, “Nick Cannon Presents: Wild ‘N Out”.
