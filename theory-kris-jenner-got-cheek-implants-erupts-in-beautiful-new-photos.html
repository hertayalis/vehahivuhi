Kris Jenner has sparked now-debunked theories she’s got cheek implants injected into her face while stood against varying spring color palettes. Her face is often subjected to fierce criticism from trolls.

The Kardashians are no stranger to online speculation. So when Kris Jenner posed in a gym top and leggings against a striking color palette of books, her cheek ‘implants’ became the talk of her fans.

Kris Jenner ‘cheek implants’ rumors

Kris Jenner is facing rumors she got cheek implants due to the defined structure of her cheekbones in recent photos. At the start of the year, Kardashian fans spotted a visual difference in her face.

Other rumors say she is on weight loss drug Ozempic, as they believe her more obvious bone structure could be down to dropping the pounds. Others say differences might be due to photo-editing apps.

GRV Media and Reality Titbit have contacted Kris Jenner’s representative for comment.

Kardashian fans react to new photos

When Kris posted new snaps of her against a color palette for an Alo advertisement, fans reacted with shock. She also posted a photo wearing a trench coat in her kitchen, and wearing an all-black outfit.

A fan said, “She is very beautiful but what has happened with her cheeks???” Another penned, “Are those cheek implants?” While one alleged, “Yes, they are, the older someone gets, the more visible they are.”

Among the speculation, Kris’ followers have her back. One said, “So what? She looks great,” with many others pitching into the conversation to agree on the Jenner’s beauty.

Inside the momager’s surgery

Kris has been candid about some of her cosmetic work, admitting to getting a boob job and a facelift and using Botox. She has denied everything else, including a nose job, and cheek implants weren’t on the list.

When it comes to Ozempic, Kris brought up the weight loss drug on The Kardashians. Khloe asked her mom if satsumas are naturally that small, asking: “Do they come this small, or do we do something?”

Kris then jokily responded: “What, you think they’re on Ozempic?” Khloe replied to her mom, “Probably,” without laughing at her mom’s joke, before Kris changed the subject.
