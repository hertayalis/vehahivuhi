About

English Actress who appeared in horror films during the 1960s and '70s and played 'Sandy' in 'The Prime of Miss Jean Brodie.'

Before Fame

She was born in Japan and lived throughout Asia with her British importer father before moving to England at the age of eight. She made her first film appearance, in 'The Innocents,' when she was eleven years old.

Trivia

She appeared in 'The Love Boat,' 'Hawaii Five-O,' and other popular television series.

Family Life

She met her Future husband, Harvey Jason, on the set of the 1970s horror film, 'Necromancy.' The couple married in 1971 and had two sons together.

Associated With

She acted with Marlon Brando and Rita Moreno in the 1967 crime thriller "The Night of the Following Day."
