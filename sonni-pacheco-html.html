Sonni Pacheco - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  112 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Movie actress famous for her relationship with Jeremy Renner. She starred in the movies The Wingman and American Pie Presents: The Book of Love. The Wingman won awards with TrindieFest, NEXTV Web Series & Indie Film Competition, the Houston Comedy Film Festival, Diff, and the Buffalo Niagara Film Festival. 

Before Fame

Sher made her film debut in 2009 in American Pie Presents: The Book of Love. 

Trivia

She is a proffesional model. She began sculpting while pregnant with her child. She posts pictures of sculptures she has made on her soniapachecoart Instagram page.

Family Life

She has a child named Ava Berlin Renner with Jeremy Renner. 

Associated With

She and Eugene Levy starred in American Pie Presents: The Book of Love.
