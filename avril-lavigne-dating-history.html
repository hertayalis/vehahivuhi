    Did Avril Lavigne date her guitarist, Jesse Colburn?

    In the early 2000s, Avril dated her rhythm guitarist, Jesse Colburn. Their romance was short-lived however, with Jesse eventually leaving the band following their breakup. At the time, Avril told MTV News: "He just wanted to go do his own thing and stuff, and I mean, that happens."

    Jesse, 41, played in Avril's band from 2002 to 2004. Elsewhere, the punk guitarist played for Canadian group which formed at Pickering High School in 1997.

    Did Avril Lavigne date Deryck Whibley?

    Avril subsequently dated Deryck Whibley for around one year, before Deryck popped the question in Venice in June 2005. They later tied the knot in an intimate ceremony in Montecito, California.

    The singer met the frontman at a bar aged 17. "I made out with him the first day I met him," she told in 2011. "He gave me my first shot of Jäger. I had a tie on—it was back in the days of the tie."

    Avril and Deryck nonetheless called it quits in September 2009 after three years of marriage. Avril filed for divorce a month later, citing irreconcilable differences. At the time of their split, she issued a statement on her website which read: "Deryck and I have been together for 6-and-a-half years. We have been friends since I was 17, started dating when I was 19, and married when I was 21. I am grateful for our time together, and I am grateful and blessed for our remaining friendship."

    How long did Brody Jenner and Avril Lavigne date?

    Avril and star Brody Jenner dated for two years between 2010 and 2012. The couple went on to get tattoos of each other's name, followed by matching lightning bolts and f-bomb inkings. Their decision to split in 2012 was reportedly a "mutual decision." And a quick glance at Twitter suggests that there was zero bad blood between the former lovers.

    Did Chad Kroeger and Avril Lavigne date?

    The songstress started dating Chad Kroeger in early 2012. The lovebirds were smitten with one another, with Chad popping the question after just six months of dating. Chad got down on one knee with a jaw-dropping 14-carat diamond ring. In an interview with Ryan Seacrest, Avril shared a glimpse inside her beau's proposal.

    "I had been making this scrapbook of my time in the studio. I walk over and he had taken a photo of himself holding the ring box and put the Polaroid in my scrapbook," Avril revealed.
