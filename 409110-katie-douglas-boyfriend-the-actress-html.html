Katie Douglas' boyfriend has become a topic of interest among fans. However, the "Ginny and Georgia" star has yet to share more about her love life. Instead, the actress has focused on opening up about her budding career and maintaining friendships.

Following a long career as an actress, Katie Douglas skyrocketed to fame after playing Abby Littman in the hit Netflix series, "Ginny and Georgia." In the show, the young star's character is faced with navigating life as a teenager.

However, fans have become curious about the actress's life off camera, wondering whether or not Katie Douglas has a boyfriend. Instead, the "Ginny and Georgia" star has been preoccupied with her career and friendships.

Katie Douglas' List of Male Friends Includes Actor Percy Hynes White

Since 2021, Douglas has shared the small screen with Percy Hynes White on "Pretty Hard Feelings." Both actors have since landed more prominent roles on Netflix, as White plays Xavier Thorpe in the hit series "Wednesday," starring Catherine Zeta-Jones, Jenna Ortega, and Riki Lindhome. Douglas said:

"We never thought that we would both end up working for Netflix; it's so funny. We're just, like, so proud of each other."

On January 3, 2023, White took to Instagram to share a picture of himself resting his head on his cast mate's shoulder as Douglas rested her hand on his head. He captioned the post in part, "A few years ago I started working on Pretty Hard Cases with my friend Katie here, and my wonderful Mom who's a creator and producer [...]."

Douglas is also friends with actor Dempsey Bryk, and in August 2022, she appeared on his YouTube channel for a video that touched on multiple topics, including acting.

Bryk and Douglas have known each other for many years, as they both featured in the 2017 series "Mary Kills People." The former has since been cast in multiple films, television shows, and shorts, and Bryk has tried his hand at voice acting in video games.

According to reports, Douglas has been private about her love life since 2021, and it has since remained unconfirmed whether or not she has a boyfriend.

In October 2020, Douglas shared polaroids of her and another male friend of hers, Toronto-based cinematographer and photographer Noah Lalonde. Since making a name for himself as a creative, Lalonde has worked with brands such as Twisted Tea, Moosehead Breweries, and Shopify, and musicians such as Timberland.

In 2022, the "Ginny and Georgia" actress took a trip to Ontario, Canada, and Lalonde showed off his photography skills when he took to Instagram to share impressively taken, colorless images of Douglas.

Katie Douglas Admitted to Having a 'Love Affair' with Acting

Douglas was a little girl when a talent agent scouted her after the actress appeared in a play for an after-school summer camp. After signing with an agency, she started landing gigs in commercials and kickstarted her modeling career. The actress said:

"[...] I'll always have a love affair with acting, but I am in university now and am grateful for a young career to be able to pay for it."

Katie Douglas on the People She Admires

According to reports, Douglas has been private about her love life since 2021, and it has since remained unconfirmed whether or not she has a boyfriend. However, the young actress has been very open about her career.

Despite the success of "Ginny and Georgia" — co-starring Brianne Howey, Antonia Gentry, and Felix Mallard — she revealed that one of her favorite projects she had worked on was the 2018 film "Level 16."

And when asked about the people she admired the most, Douglas said, "What a loaded question [...]It would feel so silly to single out the name of my favorite actor or songwriter. I have admiration for people who stand for what is important."
