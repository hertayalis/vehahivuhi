Tasha K loses more than a million after Cardi B wins the defamation lawsuit against her. What did she say about Cardi B?

Tasha K is a blogger, social media personality, and pop culture aficionado. She is widely known for her Youtube channel UnWine with Tasha K.

With over more than 1 million subscribers, her channel delivers entertainment news. Moreover discusses the hottest pop culture and celebrities.

Meanwhile, she is making headlines alongside rapper Cardi B. In November 2019, Cardi had sued the blogger for defamation and false claims.

On Jan 24, 2022, the famous rapper got victorious in the lawsuit. She has been awarded $1.25 million in damages, as per the court hearings.

The court now finds K liable for defamation, invasion of privacy, and causing emotional distress to the rapper. Continue to know more.

What Did Tasha K Say About Cardi B? Trail Video & More

Tasha K had said "slanderous and defamatory statements" about Cardi B. 

She had made false allegations on her Youtube video stating that Cardi B had worked as a prostitute.

Moreover, K defamed B by saying that, she has contracted STI's by the use of illegal substances and has cheated on her husband.

In 2018, the YouTuber also claimed that Cardi performed on “stripper stages”.

The rapper testified in the courts and had felt “extremely suicidal” over Tasha K’s claims. 

Cardi B had stated in the courts: “I felt defeated and depressed and I didn’t want to sleep with my husband.”
