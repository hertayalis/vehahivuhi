Photos Of Rapper NBA Youngboy In Jail Hits The Internet

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  289 words  |  Martina Birk

Photos of Baton Rouge rapper Kentrell DeSean Gaulden, known professionally as YoungBoy Never Broke Again (NBA Youngboy) in jail has hit the internet.

Youngboy was arrested couple of days after the Los Angeles Police Department (LAPD) invited a ‘K-9 Unit’ to help hunt down the rapper who ‘booted’ off the scene after some members of the Federal Bureau of Investigations (FBI) and the LAPD pulled over a vehicle he was traveling in.

A court of competent jurisdiction issued out an outstanding warrant for the arrest of the rapper to assist the FBI in their investigations on the warehouse raid last year during a music video shooting by the rapper where some guns and a lot of cash were seized by the FBI.

Yesterday, a court denied to release the rapper on a ‘bond’ stating the rapper is a ‘danger to the community. He is therefore to be remain the FBI custody till the appropriate time or till the case is close.

Youngboy is known for his controversial personality since joining the Hip Hop scene in 204 after releasing a couple of mixtapes till he was signed to Atlantic Records in 2017.

He was in a long ‘beef’ with the late Chicago rapper King Von until Von met his untimely death in front of a club in Atlanta after a fight with NBA Youngboy’s signed artiste Quando Rondo last November.

The fans of the rapper are reacting some photos coming in from the rapper’s prison facility proving the rapper is in good health and in good spirit.
