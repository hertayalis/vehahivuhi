Today, we’re diving into the fascinating world of Instagram and exploring the top 10 most commented posts that created waves across the platform. If you’re curious about who took the crown for the most comments, buckle up and join me on this Instagram adventure.

The Significance of Most Commented Instagram Posts

Instagram isn’t just about likes and shares; it’s a buzzing hive of comments that reflect genuine engagement and interaction. Whether it’s celebrities, influencers, brands, or everyday individuals, some posts manage to spark conversations that resonate across the vast Instagram community.

Top 10 Most Commented Post on Instagram

Let’s jump into the list and uncover the stories behind the top 10 most commented Instagram posts as of 2023:

Alexander Kopsialis (43.2 Million)

Hold on to your hats! Alexandros Kopsialis, a Greek influencer and YouTuber, stole the show with a mind-blowing 43.2 million comments on a single post. What’s the secret behind this jaw-dropping number? A giveaway! Yes, a simple yet effective strategy of offering prizes like a car, iPhone, PlayStation, and cosmetics turned this post into a viral sensation.

This incredible feat showcases the marketing prowess of social media and how giveaways can turn heads, engage audiences, and create a buzz that echoes far and wide.

Bizarrap and Paulolondra (23.2 Million)

Breaking records, Bizarrap and Paulo Londra, an Argentine record producer and DJ duo, set the Instagram stage on fire. Their post announcing a music collaboration, titled “#session23,” challenged followers to generate 23 million comments for an early release. The result? A whopping 23.2 million comments and 7 million likes, making it a spectacular success.

This post not only showcases the power of collaborations but also the ability of influencers to mobilize their audience and build anticipation through creative challenges.

MrBeast (20 Million)

Get ready for some excitement from the world of MrBeast! The American YouTuber and philanthropist celebrated his birthday with a bang – a massive $50,000 giveaway. The catch? Participants had to share the post, tag a friend, and follow MrBeast’s account. The response was overwhelming, with 20 million comments flooding in within a day.

Despite the post being temporarily removed due to Instagram policies, MrBeast’s ability to engage his audience and turn an event into a celebration is a testament to his social media influence.

XXXTentacion (10.7 Million)

Sometimes, Instagram becomes a canvas for heartfelt expressions. In the aftermath of XXXTentacion’s tragic passing, his followers poured their emotions into the comments section, accumulating a staggering 10.7 million heartfelt messages. This post stands as a tribute to the enduring impact of the talented rapper and the emotional connection he shared with his fans.

The comments serve as a digital memorial, emphasizing the everlasting influence of XXXTentacion’s music on his dedicated followers.

Andrea Stramaccioni (9.6 Million)

Enter the world of football with Andrea Stramaccioni, an Italian football manager, whose Instagram debut garnered a remarkable 9.6 million comments. Despite having just one post on his profile, the overwhelming response showcases the excitement and eagerness of fans to connect directly with a prominent figure in the football industry.

Stramaccioni’s ability to generate such engagement with a single post highlights the personal connections that social media can facilitate between public figures and their fans.

Gorgeous Armenia (6.5 Million)

Prepare to be amazed by the debut post of “gorgeous_armenia.” With a modest following, this account managed to captivate Instagram users with a visually stunning montage of Armenia, racking up an impressive 6.5 million comments. The success of this post emphasizes the power of captivating visual content in sparking engagement and igniting curiosity.

Even lesser-known accounts can make a significant impact through visually appealing and compelling posts that resonate with a global audience.

Sidhu Moose Wala (4.75 Million)

Switching gears to the vibrant world of Punjabi music, Sidhu Moose Wala, an Indian Punjabi singer, set a new record with 4.75 million comments on one Instagram post. How did he achieve this feat? By presenting a challenge to his followers – reach 5 million comments, and he would release the teaser of his highly anticipated album, “Moose Tape.”

This achievement highlights the influence of music in engaging audiences, and Sidhu Moose Wala’s ability to mobilize his fanbase for large-scale engagement campaigns.

World Record Egg (3.34 Million)

Now, prepare to be amazed by the tale of an ordinary object achieving extraordinary fame – an egg! The iconic egg photo, posted by world_record_egg, became the most-liked post on Instagram and garnered a staggering 3.34 million comments. What’s the story behind it? A simple yet powerful message challenging users to surpass the world record for likes set by Kylie Jenner.

This egg post became a symbol of unity and collective action, showcasing the potential of social media to mobilize a global community around a shared goal.

Asorloth (3.32 Million)

For soccer enthusiasts, the post from Alexander Sørloth, also known as Asorloth, stirred up emotions. The Norwegian professional football player’s post accumulated an astounding 3.32 million comments, with every single one expressing devoted Trabzonspor supporters’ desire for his return.

This post is a testament to the emotional connection that football fosters among fans and highlights the broader influence of the sport in uniting and inspiring passion worldwide.

One Direction (3.2 Million)

Attention, One Direction fans! The beloved boy band shared an animated post featuring Stewie Griffin from Family Guy, generating a massive buzz. With 3.2 million live comments, this collaboration sparked anticipation and curiosity among fans, creating a unique and memorable moment that resonated deeply.

The collaboration exemplifies the power of social media to unite fandoms and spark conversations, blending the immense popularity of One Direction with the iconic character of Stewie Griffin.

Who has the Highest Comments on Instagram Now?

As of 2024, the title for the most commented Instagram post goes to Indian celebrity mr_faisu_07, who celebrated his 25th birthday with a post that amassed over 1 million comments. With a massive following of over 36 million on Instagram, Mr. Faisu continues to capture the attention and engagement of his dedicated fanbase.

Which Celebrity has the Most Comments on Instagram 2023?

One influencer making waves on Instagram is Alexandros Kopsialis, a Greek comedic talent with over 1 million followers. Born in 1992, Alexandros uses his platform, alexandros_kopsialis, to share relatable and humorous content, resonating with fans worldwide.

While details about his personal life remain limited, his Instagram presence offers a peek into his entertaining world, engaging followers through comedic snippets and relatable posts.

In conclusion, Instagram continues to be a dynamic stage where influencers, celebrities, and everyday individuals can create moments that resonate globally. The top 10 most commented posts of 2023 showcase the diverse strategies used by creators to engage audiences, from giveaways and collaborations to heartfelt expressions and challenges. So, as you scroll through your Instagram feed, keep an eye out for the next post that might just break the comment record!
