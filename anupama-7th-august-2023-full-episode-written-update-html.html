Kavya’s Heartfelt Apology

The latest episode begins with a heartfelt moment as Kavya engages in a candid conversation with the baby. She admits her mistake and acknowledges that Vanraj is upset with her too. Kavya reflects on how in real life, adults make mistakes, and it’s the children who bear the brunt. She reassures the baby, asking for forgiveness as she’s trying her best. It’s a touching moment that tugs at the heartstrings.

Barkha’s Resentment

On the other side, Barkha expresses her deep dislike for Robin to Anupama. This animosity puzzles Anupama, and she questions Barkha’s motives. Despite Anupama’s pleas to eat, Barkha remains irate. The tension escalates as Barkha reveals her intolerance towards Robin, citing instances where Ankush was hurt. Anupama, deeply concerned for Barkha, tries to convince her that Robin is innocent and undeserving of such resentment.

A Glimmer of Hope

Anupama’s words aim to sow the seeds of understanding in Barkha’s heart. She reiterates that Robin is not at fault and deserves a fair chance. Barkha, however, remains adamant in her stance, vowing not to accept Robin. This family drama unfolds against the backdrop of a metaphorical dark night where the dawn of understanding seems distant.

Surprises and Struggles

The episode takes a lighter turn as Dimple and Samar present a new car to the family. Their excitement is palpable, but questions arise about the practicality of such a gift. Hasmukh expresses concern about managing the EMI, given their financial constraints. This gift also stirs jealousy in Paritosh, who suspects Malti Devi’s ulterior motives. Dimple playfully accuses Paritosh of jealousy, while Samar joins in the banter. This lighthearted moment is short-lived as it takes an unexpected turn.

Unforeseen Consequences

Vanraj’s anger flares as he admonishes Samar for misbehaving with elders. He emphasizes his knowledge of Paritosh being Vanraj’s favorite child. Vanraj insists that Samar apologize to Paritosh, but Samar staunchly refuses. He raises suspicions about Malti’s intentions, believing she seeks revenge against Anupama. The tension in the family thickens, leaving the audience in suspense.
