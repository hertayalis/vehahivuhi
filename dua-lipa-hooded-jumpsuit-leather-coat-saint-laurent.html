Dua Lipa Is Effortlessly Cool in a Hooded Jumpsuit and Floor-Length Leather Coat

 Posted on July 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  314 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Dua Lipa's latest look proves why she's this season's It girl.

Yesterday, the Grammy-winning singer made a fashionable arrival at Saint Laurent's fall/winter 2023 show in Paris.

Lipa's sleek, all-black ensemble put a twist on the hooded gown trend; she dressed up in a black jumpsuit with a halter neckline that twisted into a hood. She wore the piece with a black belt cinched at her waist, then layered up with a sweeping black leather trench coat. She capped the look off with shiny black pumps, black oversized sunglasses, chunky gold-and-diamond circular pendant earrings, and a bulbous gold ring.

As for her glam, the singer went for a subtle smoky eye paired with a light brown lip, plus a cherry red mani. She swept her dark hair up into a slicked-back bun.

Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

She additionally shared a behind-the-scenes look at her Saint Laurent outfit on Instagram. Next to a series of photos of herself posing in an ornate hotel room, Lipa wrote, "A perfect trip to Paris 🖤merci @anthonyvaccarello @ysl 🖤."

The singer has been taking fashion month by storm.

Last week, she attended the GCDS runway show during Milan Fashion Week in another all-black look. For that, she wore a see-through lace jumpsuit featuring a mock turtleneck, a cutout on the backside, and long sleeves that extended into gloves. Underneath, she wore a black bra and underwear, styled with a pair of black platform heels. She accessorized with a bright red heart-shaped purse and a matching mani.
