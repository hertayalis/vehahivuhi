Davide Renne: A Fashion Icon


Davide Renne will forever be remembered as a true fashion icon who made an indelible mark on the industry. His exceptional creativity and ability to capture the essence of Italian fashion set him apart as a visionary. Renne’s legacy serves as a testament to his immense talent and unwavering passion for the world of fashion.

Early Life and Passion for Fashion

From a young age, Davide Renne’s love for fashion was evident. Growing up in the picturesque town of Follonica, Italy, he was immersed in the rich culture and artistic heritage of his homeland. This upbringing nurtured his deep understanding of the creative process and ignited his passion for fashion. Renne’s journey began with his studies at the prestigious Istituto Marangoni in Milan, where he honed his skills and developed his unique style.

Rise to Prominence at Gucci

After graduating, Renne embarked on his career at Gucci, where his talent quickly propelled him to the position of Head Designer for womenswear. Renne’s designs at Gucci were a harmonious blend of traditional Italian craftsmanship and contemporary sensibilities. His bold and innovative creations, often infused with pop culture references and playful elements, captivated audiences worldwide. Renne’s runway shows were eagerly anticipated, and his designs consistently dominated the fashion scene, solidifying his status as a rising star in the industry.

Creative Director at Moschino

In October 2023, Davide Renne assumed the role of Creative Director at Moschino, following the departure of Jeremy Scott. His appointment was met with great excitement and high expectations for the future of the brand. Renne envisioned taking Moschino to new heights, combining glamour and fun while preserving its Italian heritage. Unfortunately, his untimely passing sent shockwaves throughout the fashion world, leaving behind a profound legacy that will continue to inspire generations of fashion designers and enthusiasts.
