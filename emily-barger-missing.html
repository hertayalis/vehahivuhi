UPDATE: 14-year-old Emily Barger Missing from Georgetown found safe in western Indiana

The disappearance of 14-year-old Emily Barger has left her family and authorities deeply concerned. Emily has been missing since early Monday morning, and efforts to locate her have been ongoing ever since. Police officials and the family of an Indiana teenager are asking for the public’s help in finding her, as she is believed to be in “extreme danger” after disappearing from her family home earlier this week.

Update: Emily Barger Missing

In a recent update, the Floyd County Sheriff’s Department has confirmed that the missing 14-year-old, Emily Barger, has been found safe in Attica. However, it is not clear whether the person of interest believed to be involved in her disappearance has been taken into custody. Further details regarding her safe recovery and the ongoing investigation are expected to be released soon.

Emily Barger Missing

Fourteen-year-old Emily Barger has been missing since early Monday morning, and her father reported her missing after waking up to find her gone from her bed. Emily is from Georgetown, Indiana, and was last seen around 1 a.m. on Monday, according to Indiana State Police. She is described as 5-feet tall, weighing around 85 pounds, with blonde hair and blue eyes, but officials did not know what she was wearing when she went missing. Georgetown is situated around 120 miles to the south of Indianapolis. Emily’s father reported her missing, and police are actively investigating her disappearance.

Chief Deputy Jeff Firkins with the Floyd County Sheriff’s Office said that investigators had reached out to several other agencies in connection with the ongoing search. Emily’s father, Shawn Barger, has posted several details about his daughter and the circumstances surrounding her disappearance on Facebook. He described how his daughter’s bed was empty on Monday morning, but her phone was still at home. He later wrote that he believed his daughter was with a man who possibly drives a red truck. On Wednesday, he made an emotional appeal, begging Emily to return home. The search for Emily Barger continues, and anyone with information is urged to contact the police.
