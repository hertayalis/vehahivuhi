Christina Hall teases life updates 'on the horizon' as she shares big news

 Posted on July 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  602 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

    Christina Hall has some exciting updates to share for the upcoming season of her HGTV series , which she leads and also features appearances from her husband Josh Hall.

    The 40-year-old TV star and real estate savant took to social media to reveal that in the next few months, leading into summer, she was devoted to filming episodes for the show's fifth season.

    The fourth season of the HGTV series premiered in December 2022 and wrapped in September 2023, while the spin-off was announced at the same time.

    A fifth season of was ordered last July, and the TV star promised her fans that there was a lot to come on that front, teasing some news to arrive soon.

    Sharing a photograph of herself standing next to a beautifully (and presumably) remodeled bathroom space, Christina penned: "The kind of bathroom that makes you want to get all dolled up."
