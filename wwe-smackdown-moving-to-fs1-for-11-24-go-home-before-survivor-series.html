WWE SmackDown Moving To FS1 For 11/24 Go-Home Before Survivor Series

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  276 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

WWE will present Survivor Series next week on November 25th in Chicago’s Allstate Arena. The go-home episode of SmackDown before the big event will have a special presentation on a different network home.

Ringside News has comprehensive coverage of WWE SmackDown’s November 17th episode. It was quite a show, and it also started off with a bit of a surprise.

During WWE SmackDown this week, it was announced that next week will see WWE SmackDown air on FS1. This is a usual replacement for FOX when there is a programming issue.

They already booked Judgment Day vs The Street Profits for the Undisputed Tag Team Titles next week. Montez Ford won a triple threat match over Pretty Deadly and the Brawling Brutes. After The Street Profits won, Bobby Lashley, who was shown watching the match with B-Fab backstage during the contest, came out to celebrate with them.

We will have to see what else WWE books for SmackDown next week. They will have to book quite a stacked show if they want to maintain a viewership over 2 million on FS1, because shows on that channel rarely do much above 1.2 million.
